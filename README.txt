PhytoScan is a single-file HTML app powered by Google Gemini Vision API. Upload plant close-ups or aerial forest images to get instant AI analysis: species ID, health status, growth stage, care tips, canopy cover, NDVI estimates, and actionable recommendations. Zero backend - pure client-side.
